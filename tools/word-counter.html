About Word & Character Counter

This free online tool counts words, characters, sentences, paragraphs, and lines in your text. It also estimates reading and speaking time, and shows your most-used keywords. All processing happens in your browser.

Features

  • Real-time word, character, and sentence counting
  • Characters with and without spaces
  • Paragraph and line counting
  • Reading time estimate (~238 words/minute average)
  • Speaking time estimate (~150 words/minute average)
  • Top keyword frequency analysis

Common Use Cases

  • Checking essay or article word counts for assignments
  • Staying within social media character limits (Twitter: 280, LinkedIn: 3000)
  • Estimating presentation speaking time
  • SEO content length optimization
  • Analyzing keyword density in content

FAQ

How is reading time calculated?

Reading time is based on an average reading speed of 238 words per minute (the average for adult readers according to research). Speaking time uses 150 words per minute.

How are keywords determined?

The tool counts word frequency, excluding common stop words (the, a, is, are, etc.) and words shorter than 3 characters. The top 10 most frequent words are shown.
