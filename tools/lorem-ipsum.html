About Lorem Ipsum Generator

This free online tool generates Lorem Ipsum placeholder text for your designs, mockups, wireframes, and prototypes. All processing happens in your browser.

Features

  • Generate paragraphs, sentences, or individual words
  • Customize the amount of text generated
  • Option to start with the classic "Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet..."
  • Copy to clipboard or download as a text file
  • Word and character count display

What is Lorem Ipsum?

Lorem Ipsum is dummy text used in the printing and typesetting industry since the 1500s. It is derived from sections 1.10.32 and 1.10.33 of "de Finibus Bonorum et Malorum" by Cicero, written in 45 BC.

FAQ

Is Lorem Ipsum real Latin?

It's derived from Latin, but the text has been altered and scrambled over the centuries so it doesn't form coherent Latin sentences anymore.

When should I use placeholder text?

Use Lorem Ipsum during the design phase when you need to show text layout but don't have final copy. Replace it with real content before going to production.
