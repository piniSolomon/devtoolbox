What is Lorem Ipsum?

Lorem Ipsum is placeholder text used in the design and typesetting industry since the 1500s. It originates from sections 1.10.32 and 1.10.33 of "de Finibus Bonorum et Malorum" (The Extremes of Good and Evil) by Cicero, written in 45 BC. The text was popularized in the 1960s with Letraset sheets and later by desktop publishing software like Aldus PageMaker.

FAQ

Is Lorem Ipsum real Latin?

It's based on real Latin from Cicero's writings, but the commonly used version has been altered, with words changed and removed, making it nonsensical. It reads like Latin but doesn't translate to anything meaningful.

Why not just use random text?

Lorem Ipsum has a natural distribution of letters and word lengths that mimics real language. Random text or repeated characters like "aaa bbb ccc" don't give an accurate impression of how final content will look in a layout.
